Our Story

Built on curiosity.
Driven by craft.

Krishan Kumar started as a curiosity about how things work on the internet. That curiosity turned into a career building web experiences that are fast, accessible, and genuinely useful.

Every project is approached with the same principle: move fast enough to learn, but build carefully enough to last. No shortcuts on quality. No compromise on performance.

From early-stage startups to established brands, the work spans strategy, design, and engineering — always in service of a clear goal.
